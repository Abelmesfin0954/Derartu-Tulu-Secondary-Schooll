Process Council Committee

የሂደት ምክር ቤት ኮሚቴ

Process Council Committee:The Process Council Committee aims to optimize administrative workflows, ensuring efficiency and effectiveness in school operations through collaboration, standardization, and continuous improvement efforts.

Curriculum Committee

የስርዓተ ትምህርት ኮሚቴ

Curriculum Committee: This committee is responsible for reviewing and evaluating the school curriculum to ensure alignment with educational standards, addressing any gaps or areas for improvement, and recommending updates or revisions as needed to enhance the quality of instruction and students. learning outcomes.

Question and Answer Committee

ጥያቄ እና መልስ ኮሚቴ

Question and Answer Committee::This committee facilitates communication between students and teachers by collecting questions from students, organizing Q&A sessions with teachers, and ensuring that students receive clear and accurate information regarding academic matters, school policies, and procedures.

Social Committee

ማህበራዊ ኮሚቴ

Social Committee :is tasked with fostering a vibrant and inclusive school community by organizing events, activities, and initiatives that promote social interaction, cultural exchange, and student engagement.

Basic teachers committee

መሰረታዊ መምህራን ካሚቴ

Basic Teachers Committee:The Basic Teachers Committee focuses on supporting and empowering teachers by addressing their professional development needs, providing resources and training opportunities, and promoting mentorship among faculty members to enhance teaching effectiveness and student engagement.

T.M.A. Committee

ተ.ሙ.ማ ኮሚቴ

T.M.A.(Teaching, Monitoring, and Assessment) Committee:committee oversees the teaching, monitoring, and assessment practices within the school, ensuring that instructional methods are effective, student progress is monitored regularly, and assessment strategies are fair, valid, and aligned. with learning objectives.

Disciplinary Committee

የዲሲፕሊን ኮሚቴ

The Disciplinary Committee:maintains a safe and respectful learning environment by addressing student misconduct, enforcing school rules, and implementing appropriate interventions to promote positive behavior and responsible citizenship.

W.T.M. Committee

ወ.ተ.መ ኮሚቴ

W.T.M. (Waste, Transportation, and Maintenance) Committee:committee oversees waste management, transportation services, and maintenance of school facilities and infrastructure, ensuring cleanliness, safety, and functionality to support a conducive learning environment for students and staff.

Promotion Committee

የደረጃ እድገት ኮሚቴ

Promotion Committee:The Promotion Committee coordinates promotional activities and events to celebrate student achievements, recognize academic excellence, and foster school spirit and community pride through initiatives such as award ceremonies, talent shows, and cultural festivals.

Quality Committee

የጥራት ኮሚቴ

Quality Committee:The Quality Committee focuses on continuous improvement and quality assurance across all aspects of school operations, including teaching and learning, administrative processes, facilities management, and student services, by implementing best practices, conducting evaluations, and soliciting feedback from stakeholders.
